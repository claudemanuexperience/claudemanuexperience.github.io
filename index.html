About the Artist

Claude Manu ― known to friends as Manu ― is a talented young artist from Brazil. Among her many virtues as a painter, she is especially known for her depiction of the mar descendente. Her work has garnered a worldwide audience, with famed American art critic William Roque writing: "every stroke of her brush will inspire yet another generation". Though the extent of her published works are limited to these as of today, we hope to see far more in the coming years.
